## 2. SYSTEM ARCHITECTURE (HOW IT WORKS)

### Phase I: Identity Initialization (USERSIDE)
The business enters the system through a minimalist onboarding terminal. 
- **Business Identity**: Anchoring the name, tagline, and contact vectors.
- **The Brain (Knowledge Base)**: Feeding the AI raw data—FAQs, PDFs, and strategic text. Unlike basic bots, this system uses multimodal vision to "see" the internal data of uploaded documents.
- **The Inventory (Catalog)**: Structuring services into high-contrast visual cards with definitive pricing and AI-triggered follow-up logic.

### Phase II: The Front-Door Operative (CLIENTSIDE)
An anonymous, terminal-style chat interface deployed for the end-user.
- **Contextual Intelligence**: The AI assumes the persona of an elite business consultant, not a chatbot. It cross-references the Catalog and Knowledge Base in real-time.
- **Multimodal Vision**: The AI processes PDF data injected directly into the conversation stream for precision accuracy.
- **Lead Extraction**: Automically identifying names and WhatsApp numbers from natural language and piping them into the CRM.

---

## 3. THE STACK: PRECISION ENGINEERING
Built for speed, aesthetics, and psychological impact.
- **Engine**: React + Vite (Static-speed delivery).
- **Core Intelligence**: Gemini 3 Flash (High-velocity multimodal processing).
- **Hard Drive**: Supabase (PostgreSQL for real-time lead and identity persistence).
- **Kinetic Design**: Motion (Framer Motion) for liquid transitions.
- **Visuals**: Vanilla CSS and Tailwind for the "Dark Power" aesthetic (High contrast, glassmorphism).

---

## 4. THE GAP: ROADMAP TO FULL SAAS
To evolve from a high-end tool into a global SaaS platform, the following architectural components are required:
1. **Multi-Tenancy Authentication**: Transitioning from "anonymous onboarding" to a managed User/Organization model via Supabase Auth (Email/Google).
2. **Subscription Infrastructure**: Integration of Stripe Billing for tier-based access to AI depth and lead limits.
3. **Widget Deployment Engine**: A generator that outputs a single `<script>` tag for businesses to embed the ChatWidget on any external website.
4. **Lead Analytics Dashboard**: Heatmaps and conversion metrics to visualize "Stolen Life" reclaimed by the AI.
5. **Multi-Bot Management**: Ability for one user to manage multiple business identities from a single command center.
